Dynamic pricing, weekly human review

PriceLabs as the engine, Tuesday/Wednesday manual override each week. We set minimum-price floors and last-minute discount rules to fix shoulder-season performance.

Listing optimization

Pro photography, SEO-tuned titles and descriptions, amenity callouts that match what's actually booking in your submarket. Multi-platform syndication.

24/7 guest communication

Median reply time under 10 minutes. Owner-facing and guest-facing comms live in Hospitable with message routing across the team.

Turnover & cleaning

Vetted cleaning partners via TurnoverBnB, with post-clean inspections. Linen restocking, amenity resets, and consumables handled.

Maintenance network

Trusted local vendors for HVAC, plumbing, electrical, and handyman work. Flat, transparent markup — no surprise fees.

Reviews as a ranking engine

Reviews are a non-negotiable ranking signal per Bodhi's SEO framework. We treat review generation as a core operational habit.

Trust-accounted statements

Owner payouts by the 10th. Pass-throughs (cleaning, utilities, supplies) shown separately from management commission. QuickBooks-clean.

Tax & permit compliance

TDT remittance (Palm Beach County), occupancy tax, and platform pass-throughs. We'll walk you through permit requirements in your market.

Design consultation

Single-accent-color rooms, matching hardware finishes, amenity differentiation. We source from Wayfair, Amazon, and Habitat for Humanity at ~$10/sqft.
