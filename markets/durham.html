Demand drivers

What fills Durham STRs
year-round.

Duke & Duke Medical

Family weekends, parent visits, medical rotations, and patient family stays drive a consistent booking floor — especially walking distance from campus.

DPAC & the American Tobacco Campus

Broadway tours, concerts, and corporate events at DPAC fuel premium weekend pricing. We tune nightly rates to the DPAC calendar every week.

Research Triangle Park

Corporate relocations, interview visits, and short-term contractor stays — RTP is minutes from Durham and keeps weekday occupancy up.

Duke basketball & commencement

Cameron Crazies, tournament weekends, and graduation events produce 4–6 hard-peak weekends we price separately every year.

The food scene

Bull City's restaurant reputation draws weekend travelers from Raleigh, Chapel Hill, and even Charlotte. We call that out in listings.

RDU airport proximity

Quick RDU access makes Durham listings competitive with Raleigh-focused travelers who find our nightly rates more favorable.
