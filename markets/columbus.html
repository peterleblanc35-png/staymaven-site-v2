Demand drivers

What fills Columbus STRs
all 12 months.

OSU home games

Six to eight peak weekends a year we price separately. Game-day ADRs can run 3–5x a typical Saturday.

Short North nightlife

Restaurant scene, galleries, and events keep Short North listings in the top performance band year-round.

Intel & tech relocations

The new Intel plant, Amazon data centers, and ongoing tech hiring produce 2–6 week relocation stays with premium ADRs.

Medical tourism

Ohio State Wexner Medical Center and Nationwide Children's Hospital drive family stays with steady mid-week occupancy.

Convention center events

The Greater Columbus Convention Center pulls 2M+ annual attendees. We calendar the big ones and price premium.

German Village charm

Cobblestone streets and brick architecture make German Village a premium submarket that books faster than its comps.
