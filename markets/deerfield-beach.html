Demand drivers

What fills South Florida coastal STRs.

Snowbird season (Nov–Apr)

30- and 60-night stays drive premium ADRs. We build dedicated monthly-rate channels for this season and convert them to repeat bookings.

Spring break

March brings peak demand — we price aggressively and require stricter screening to protect the property. Justin walks every check-in personally in spring.

Boat show & events

Fort Lauderdale International Boat Show, Art Basel Miami overflow, and Hard Rock Hollywood events push premium weekend ADRs.

Medical tourism

Delray Medical, Boca Raton Regional, and Holy Cross drive family stays with predictable mid-week occupancy year-round.

Walkable beach life

Deerfield's walkable waterfront makes it rare on the Atlantic. We position listings to emphasize pier proximity and walkable dining.

FLL & PBI airports

Quick access to both Fort Lauderdale and Palm Beach airports expands the booking funnel beyond single-market dependence.
