Raleigh's strength is its diversity of demand: NC State, RDU airport, downtown corporate travel, and a booming relocation market. We tune positioning and pricing to whichever your property pulls.

The market

Four demand engines

Demand drivers

What fills Raleigh STRs
every week.

NC State

Home games, family weekends, and commencement season produce 3–5 premium peak weekends we price separately every year.

RDU airport proximity

Corporate travelers, conference attendees, and one-night layovers find well-priced downtown stays more appealing than hotel chains.

Downtown events

Red Hat Amphitheater, PNC Arena hockey, Duke Energy Center concerts — we calendar these and price accordingly.

Tech relocations

Raleigh leads the Southeast for corporate relocation. 2–6 week stays from relocating employees are a quiet but consistent revenue band.

Medical tourism

UNC REX and Duke Raleigh Hospital drive extended family stays with premium average daily rates when listings are hospital-adjacent.

North Hills & shopping

Midtown Raleigh weekend demand keeps North Hills properties booked through shoulder seasons when downtown dips.
