About Stay Maven

Built by operators,
for operators.

Six years of hosting, a lot of 2am phone calls, and one strong belief: the best short-term rental managers are the ones who've actually done it themselves.

Our story

Stay Maven started with
a spare bedroom.

Michael Setuain listed his first spare room on Airbnb in 2019. By 2021 he'd learned enough — the hard way — to start managing other owners' properties in Durham. Today Stay Maven operates a boutique portfolio across three states, each run by a named principal who actually lives in the market.

We built the company we wished existed when we were the ones cleaning the place ourselves at midnight. A real operator on-site. Transparent trust accounting. Month-to-month contracts. Pricing that gets reviewed every single week by a human, not just an algorithm.

"We set a 19% management fee floor — not because we can't do the math, but because below that, something has to give. Usually it's the owner's revenue or the guest's experience."

We're not trying to be the biggest STR manager. We're trying to be the best one in the markets we serve, and to earn our owners' renewal every single month.

How we operate

Five principles
we don't compromise on.

Trust accounting is a legal standard, not a bookkeeping choice.

Owner payouts happen before our draws. Pass-throughs are separated from our commission on every statement. Always.

Reviews are a ranking signal, not an afterthought.

We treat review generation as an operational habit. It's why we rank, it's why we book, and it's why owners trust us with the next property.

Pricing gets reviewed weekly — by a human.

Algorithms handle the base. A human handles the exceptions: shoulder season, orphan nights, events, weather. That's where the revenue lift lives.

Design cohesion drives performance.

Single accent color per property, matching hardware, amenity differentiation. We advise owners the same way we'd advise ourselves.

We earn renewal every 30 days — that's why contracts are month-to-month.

If we're not pulling our weight, an owner should be able to leave in 30 days. We operate as if that's always the case. So far, they stay.
