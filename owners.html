Common questions

What owners usually ask first.

What does the 20% management fee cover?

Everything except owner-responsible pass-throughs like cleaning (paid by guest), utilities, and property-specific maintenance. The 20% covers listing, pricing, guest communication, reviews, turnover coordination, vendor management, monthly statements, and 24/7 support. We publish our fee floor of 19% — we don't negotiate below that because we won't compromise operations to win a property.

How long is the contract?

Month-to-month with 30-day notice, in compliance with NC law. We believe you should stay because we're earning it, not because of a contract. We operate every market this way, even where longer contracts are legal.

What's your revenue lift vs. self-management?

Portfolio-wide we average 18–32% more gross revenue than owners were generating before switching. The swing comes from three things: tighter pricing (weekly manual review, not just algorithm), better listing optimization, and professional guest messaging that converts inquiries to bookings. We show you the math before you sign.

Do you handle taxes, permits, and insurance?

We handle TDT remittance in Palm Beach County, occupancy tax in NC where applicable, and Airbnb/VRBO passthroughs. We'll also coordinate with your insurance (we recommend Proper) and walk you through local permit requirements. Rules vary by market — that's why having a local operator matters.

What if I want to stay in the property myself?

Your property, your rules. Block off your own dates in the owner portal whenever you want. Many of our owners use their homes several weeks per year.
